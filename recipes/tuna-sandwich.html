Lasagna

Description

A tuna sandwich is more often a miss than a hit because it either tastes like crap or gives --me-- a stomachache. It's alright otherwise though.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Canned tuna

Steps

  1. Prepare the tuna however you like
  2. Put the tuna a slice of bread
  3. Put the other slice of bread on the tuna
  4. Enjoy
