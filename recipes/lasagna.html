Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a good food. It is made of pasta.

To make lasagna you will need sauce and meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground beef
  • 12oz shredded mozarella cheese
  • 2lb lasagna pasta
  • 1 can tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Put the beef on half the pasta.
  2. Slather tomato sauce on the other half.
  3. Sandwich the beef with the two halves.
  4. Put your mozarella cheese on the top.
  5. Leave in oven 350F 45 min. Let sit for three minutes while hot.
